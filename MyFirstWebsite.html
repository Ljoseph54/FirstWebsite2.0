#6: Black Desert Online

Black Desert Online is a Korean MMORPG developed by Pearl Abyss and published by Kakao Games. It is a sandbox oriented game that involves action packed combat and an extensive crafting system. It features an open world which is explorable by both horses and boats. It has intense PVP from small scale 1v1 fights to large guild wars.

#5: Neverwinter

Neverwinter is a gothic MMORPG developed by Cryptic Studios. It features realistic combat and player made quests. It has a intense crafting system and thousands of different weapons.

#4: Dual Universe

Dual Universe is currently in the Pre-Alpha stage. It is developed by Novaquark, and it is a open world Sci-Fi MMORPG. It allows palyers to build their own ships and mine anywhere on the planet.

#3: EVE Online

Eve Online is a space based MMORPG developed by CCP. It has thousands of galaxies and features a complex player based market. It has pvp, pve, mining, exploration, and much more. It is made for players to do what they want to do and not follow a story line.

#2: Elder Scrolls Online

ESO takes place in the Elder Scrolls universe. It is developed by Zenimax and it is very realistic. It has a beautiful open world and features all aspects of a typical MMORPG. It has a unique first person combat that very few MMO's have.

#1: World of Warcraft

WOW is a classic MMORPG developed by Blizzard Studios. It has a huge world and features many dungeons and PVP combat. It has thousands of quests and endless hours of content. It has a slight cartoon feel to it which makes it more relaxing.
